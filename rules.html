  • Disqualification for severe or repeated fouls.

No Contest Rule

A fight becomes No Contest when cheating occurs, a severe rule violation changes the fight, the fight is stopped unfairly, admin decision requires it, or outside interference affects the outcome.

No Contest fights do not count as wins or losses and are ignored by rankings.

Fight Reversal Rule

Admin can reverse fights when the wrong winner was selected, cheating is discovered, a rule violation was missed, or a system error affected the result. Reversed fights are completely removed from ranking calculations.
