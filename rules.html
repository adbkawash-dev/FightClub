📜 RULES

The following acts constitute fouls in a contest or exhibition and may result in penalties or disqualification at the discretion of the admin/referee.

  • Butting with the head
  • Eye gouging of any kind
  • Biting or spitting at an opponent
  • Fish hooking
  • Hair pulling
  • Spiking an opponent on head or neck
  • Strikes to spine or back of head
  • Throat strikes or grabbing trachea
  • Fingers toward eyes/face
  • Downward elbow strike (12 to 6)
  • Groin attacks
  • Kicking/kneeing grounded opponent’s head
  • Stomping a grounded opponent
  • Holding gloves or shorts
  • Holding fence/ropes
  • Small joint manipulation
  • Throwing opponent out of area
  • Inserting fingers into cuts or orifices
  • Clawing, pinching, twisting flesh
  • Timidity or avoiding contact
  • Abusive language in fight area
  • Ignoring referee instructions
  • Unsportsmanlike conduct causing injury
  • Attacking after bell
  • Attacking during break
  • Attacking under referee control
  • Corner interference

⚖️ Penalties

  • Fouls may result in point deduction
  • Multiple fouls may lead to disqualification
  • Only admin/referee can confirm fouls

❌ No Contest Rule

A fight can be marked as NO CONTEST if:

  • Severe rule violations happen
  • Cheating is confirmed
  • Fight is stopped unfairly
  • Admin decides result is invalid
